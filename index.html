Video Links Extractor

How to Use

1. Log in to your account and navigate to your video page.

2. Use the bookmarklet (provided below) to capture the page source automatically.

3. The video links will be extracted and displayed below.

4. Use the action buttons to open, share, copy, or export the links.

Click this button and copy the bookmarklet code to your bookmarks bar.
